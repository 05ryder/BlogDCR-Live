About BlogDCR

Broadcasting from Robinson Hall since 1958, WEBDCR has been the voice of Dartmouth College and the Upper Valley. We're student-run, independent, and dedicated to showcasing the best in music, culture, and community.

Our Mission

BlogDCR serves as both the digital home of WEBDCR and a platform for student voices. We believe in the power of college radio to discover new music, foster community, and provide a space for creative expression.

From intimate live sessions in our Robinson Hall studio to in-depth interviews with touring artists, we're committed to bringing you authentic music journalism and exclusive content.

Get Involved

Whether you want to host a show, write about music, or help behind the scenes, there's a place for you at WEBDCR.

Join us for our weekly meetings, submit content through our platform, or simply tune in and become part of the WEBDCR community.
